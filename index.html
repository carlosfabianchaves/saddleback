It's Our Time Again

We are excited to announce that this winter,
Saddleback Mountain reopens for all to enjoy.

We’ll have brand new chairlifts, significantly improved snowmaking, a renovated base lodge and ticket prices aimed at making the Saddleback experience more accessible than ever.

Here’s what won’t change: A friendly, welcoming vibe, a wide diversity of terrain for all abilities, and a whole lot of love for our community. We can’t wait to ski and ride with you again.
